Yasamin Tabatabaee

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign advised by Prof. Tandy Warnow. I received my undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology, Iran in 2021.

I am interested in algorithmic problems in computational biology. Much of my research has been focused on phylogenomics and species tree estimation.
