Yasamin Tabatabaee

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign advised by Prof. Tandy Warnow. Prior to this, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology, Iran in 2021 and my M.S. degree from UIUC in 2023.

My research is focused on algorithmic problems in computational biology, in particular phylogenomics and species tree estimation.
