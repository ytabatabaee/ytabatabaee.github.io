Yasamin Tabatabaee

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign advised by Prof. Tandy Warnow. Prior to this, I received a B.S. degree in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology, Iran in 2021 and an M.S. degree in Computer Science from UIUC in 2023.

My research is focused on algorithmic problems in computational biology, in particular phylogenomics and species tree estimation.
