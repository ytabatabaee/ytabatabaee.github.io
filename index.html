Yasamin Tabatabaee

I am a Computer Science PhD candidate at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign advised by Prof. Tandy Warnow. Prior to this, I received a B.S. degree in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology in 2021 and an M.S. degree in Computer Science from UIUC in 2023. During summer 2024, I was a visiting scholar at UCSD working with Prof. Siavash Mirarab.

My main research is on algorithmic problems in computational biology. In particular, my PhD dissertation has been focused on developing algorithms and computational tools for estimating the evolutionary history of species from genome-scale datasets (phylogenomics). I have also worked on community detection and clustering on large networks.
